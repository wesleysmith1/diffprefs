STAGE 2

In Stage 2, your task is again to count the number of ones in a grid, this time in a 15-minute interval. You will be paid for each grid you count correctly. Again you are welcome to surf the Internet in a separate tab at any time. You do not earn money by surfing the Internet. In this stage, you are not required to complete a specific number of grids like you were in Stage 1.

The private contents of the envelope (again, you are not able to open yet!) will affect your Stage 2 earnings. If the envelope contains a green card, you will earn $0.15 for each grid counted correctly. If it contains a red card, each grid correctly counted will pay $0.02. You will not know the contents of the envelope until after Stage 2.

Information about the color of the card

Before you see any grids, you will receive information about the color of the card in your envelope. The information will be correct 60% of the time and wrong 40% of the time.

This means that if you have a green card in the envelope, the information will tell you that you have a green card 60% of the time. And if you have a red card, the information will tell you that you have a red card 60% of the time.

You will open the envelope at the end of Stage 2. Therefore, you will not know the color of your card until we complete Stage 2.

You can change red card earnings

You will have the option to use $3.80 of your earnings to increase the amount you would earn per grid solved if the card in the envelope happens to be red. If you use the $3.80, the grids are worth $0.05 each if you have a red card verses $0.02 if you do not use the $3.80. The grid earnings remain at $0.15 if you have a green card, with or without using the $3.80. Using the $3.80 will subtract $3.80 from your Stage 2 earnings.

STAGE 3

During Stage 3, you will be making a choice about playing a lottery. This task will be described on the computer screen when we all begin Stage 3.

Finally, you will be answering some basic survey questions at the end of the experiment.

On the next screen, you will answer some questions to ensure your understanding of the experiment.
