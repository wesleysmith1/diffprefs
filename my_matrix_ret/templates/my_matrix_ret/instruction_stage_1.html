During the first stage of the experiment, you can earn money by performing a computerized task. The task consists of a grid with rows of zeros and ones. An example is shown in the figure below. Your task is to count the number of ones in the grid. You will earn $0.15 per grid counted correctly. In this stage you are required to solve 28 grids and earn $4.20. This should take you about 5 minutes. If at any point you would like to surf the Internet in a separate tab during this stage, go to a browser and enjoy. Remember to complete 28 grids.
