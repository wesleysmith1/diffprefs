In stage two, your task is again to count the number of ones in a grid, this time in a 15-minute interval. You will be paid for each grid you count correctly. Again you are welcome to surf the Internet in a separate tab at any time. You do not earn money by surfing the Internet.

The private contents of the envelope (please DO NOT open yet!) will affect your stage two earnings. If the envelope contains a green card, you will earn $0.15 for each grid counted correctly. If it contains a red card, each grid correctly counted will pay $0.02. You will not know the contents of the envelope until after stage two.

{% endblock %} {% block title %} Information about the color of the card {% endblock %}

Before you see any grids, you will receive information about the color of the card in your envelope. The information will be correct 60% of the time and wrong 40% of the time.

This means that if you have a green card in the envelope, the information will tell you that you have a green card 60% of the time. And if you have a red card, the information will tell you that you have a red card 60% of the time.

You will open the envelope at the end of stage two. Therefore, you will not know the color of your card until we complete stage two.
